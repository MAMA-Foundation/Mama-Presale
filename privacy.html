PRIVACY POLICY MAMA Token Presale

Last Updated: August 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and store information during your use of the $MAMA presale platform.

DATA WE COLLECT

We may collect and store the following data:

  • Your connected wallet address
  • Referral code used or shared
  • Amount of SOL transferred
  • Solana transaction signature
  • Timestamps and presale metadata
  • Non-identifying device/browser metadata (via analytics)

We do not collect names, emails, or personally identifiable information.

HOW WE USE DATA

We use the collected data to:

  • Verify and process presale transactions
  • Attribute referrals and calculate rewards
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Analyze platform performance and improve the experience

DATA STORAGE

Referral and transaction data may be stored in CSV format or in cloud databases (e.g. Supabase, Firebase). Data is retained only as long as necessary for reward distribution and auditability.

SHARING

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except:

  • As required by applicable law or regulation
  • To enforce our Terms or protect our rights

COOKIES / LOCAL STORAGE

We use localStorage to store your referral code (if present) and avoid repeated prompts. We may also use privacy-first analytics tools (e.g. Plausible) to monitor site usage without tracking personal identities.

YOUR RIGHTS

Since we do not store personally identifiable data, most data cannot be linked back to you. If you believe your wallet address was used fraudulently, contact us via the on-site support link.

CONTACT

For questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out via the official MAMA website or verified communication channels.
